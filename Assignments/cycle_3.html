Canvas

I based my sticker off of a Komory Bat from Kingdom Hearts, which I free handed based off of a reference. I simplified the design by sticking to three colors.

After sketching it out, I turned that layer transparent and drew over it.

I hid that first layer and drew over the sketch to create a second layer.

I did the same for the last layer, which allowed for me to cut out three different colors.

After cutting out my stickers, I weeded them and used transfer tape to layer the vinyl on top of itself. I started with using the base of the bat, then layered the black wings, ears, mouth and legs, and finally added the pink details on top.

Finally, I kept the sticker on a piece of the cut vinyl backing after removing it from the transfer tape.
