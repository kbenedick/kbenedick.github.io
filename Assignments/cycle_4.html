For this cycle, I designed a basic circuit on Tinkercad that makes 3 LEDs light up at different times. I used Arduino, 7 wires, 3 resistors at 220 ohm, a Breadboard, and 3 LEDs.

For the code, I set the three pins 9, 10, and 11 to either high or low after waiting 400 or 600 milliseconds in order to make the LEDs light up at different times.

After creating both the circuit and code on Tinkercad, I downloaded the code onto an Arduino and assembled the circuit using materials in the lab. I was able to make the LEDs light up at different times as I wanted, and after running it for a few moments, I disassembled it and put all the materials away.
